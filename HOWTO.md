TRANSPORT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
(JAVA OOP CONCEPTS IN PRACTICE)
This is a Transport System, created with Java OOP concepts to provide a safe and reliable system to the Transport Businesses.
This project demonstrates how different components of a transport system interact through classes,inheritance, interfaces and the methods that are implemented While applying the OOP Concepts like Encapsulation, Abstraction, Inheritance, Method Overriding and constructors.

The users of the system include:

1. The passengers - booking services, payment and sending feedback 
2. The drivers - viewing salary and their working dates and accepting the parcels
3. Those sending parcels to different places(Parcel Services)
4. Payment Services, both for the parcels and the fare(price)

The exception applied is the try-catch excemption.
 This makes sure that the system or the program does not crash at any point, especially when the passenger is entering his or her details when:

 1. Booking(destination) - name,id or even phone number
 2. Paying for the services(price) - fare to the destination

 The try-catch exception is only applied to the passenger.
